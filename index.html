About

At Beaus Bonbons, using the best ingredients is only the first step in the creation of our exquisite selection of confections. Our passion lies in creating beautiful unique designs from our many delicious recipes.

Products

Try our scrumptious white chocolate mousse drops, or our gourmet dusted marshmallows made daily. knowing that our confection’s pleasures go beyond that of taste, he uses it as a canvas to make it a feast for your eyes. Painting with cocoa butter, our talented candy makers employ dozens of techniques to achieve unique, incomparable designs.

  • Marshmallows

    Our powder dusted specialty marshmallows are the perfect ingredient for an unforgettable gift for any occasion. They come in blue, pink, cocoa, yellow and rainbow.

  • Macaroons

    Our macarons are made fresh and come in a wide variety of flavors! Our top seller is the chocolate fudge macaron, but there are many more to choose from! Including strawberry shortcake, raspberry kiss, white chocolate hazelnut, and lemon poppy seed bliss.

  • Mousse Bites

    The essential to any box of confections- these delicate bites are akin to a truffle, but lighter and fluffier. They’re guaranteed to tantalize the palette and make gorgeous party pieces.
